## Strategic Assessment

**Opportunity**: Very high. 18,100 SV cluster with effectively zero KD. The SERP is dominated by calculator tools, and our strongest competitor (avvocatoandreani) has a significant moat via 3,575 reviews but zero editorial content, no FAQ schema, and poor technical SEO.

**Differentiation angles**:
1. Calculator + deep editorial (most competitors are tool-only OR article-only)
2. Historical rates table (2002-2026) — miolegale.it ranks separately for this
3. Two-mode calculator: D.Lgs. 231/2002 (transazioni commerciali) + art. 1224 c.c. (tra privati)
4. Schema markup (WebApplication + FAQPage + HowTo) — none of the competitors have this
5. "Fonti e Riferimenti" with Normattiva links — E-E-A-T advantage
6. Cross-link to existing calcolo-interessi-legali page — topical authority

**Risk**: AI Overview already provides the formula and current rate. Need to offer value beyond what the AI Overview covers (interactive multi-period calculator, historical rates, edge cases).
