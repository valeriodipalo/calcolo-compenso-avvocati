## Strategic Assessment

- **Opportunity level**: **Very High**. 33,100 SV primary with low-to-medium KD. Primary competitor (avvocatoandreani.it) has weak content depth — a legacy .php page with minimal editorial. Namirial and TeamSystem have no calculator. We can dominate by combining a working multi-aliquota calculator + deep editorial + authoritative source citations.
- **Differentiation angles**:
  1. **Multi-aliquota calculator** with all 5 aliquote current (4%, 5%, 10%, 22%) + reversible mode (scorporo ↔ aggiungi IVA)
  2. **Contextual aliquota suggestions** by product/service category (alimentari, edilizia, avvocati, etc.)
  3. **Legal avvocato-specific angle** — the "scorporo IVA e CPA" cluster (260 SV) and "scorporo iva avvocato" (90 SV) fit our niche perfectly
  4. **Historical aliquote IVA table** (from 4% 1973 → 22% 2013) — unique depth no competitor has
  5. **Normattiva-linked sources** (DPR 633/1972 art. 16, art. 10, TABELLA A) — E-E-A-T trust signal
- **Risks**:
  - AI Overview active on many PAA queries — mitigate with concise "passage-ready" answers under 40 words
  - "calcola iva" cluster (40,500 SV) has SaaS-tier competitors (Qonto, SumUp, Fiscozen) — we won't rank #1 there, but we can capture 4,400 SV "calcolo iva 22" variants
  - Money.it and avvocatoandreani.it have domain authority we lack — need exceptional content depth to compete
