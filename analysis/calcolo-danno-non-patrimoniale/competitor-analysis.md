## Key Findings

### Andreani (#1 for primary keyword)
- **Strength**: Calculator with embeddable widget, ratings (4.6/1170), ranks #1 for many keywords
- **Weakness**: Very thin content (~400 words of explanatory text), NO FAQ, NO schema markup, NO normativa links, NO deep legal content
- **Opportunity**: They split micropermanenti and macropermanenti into separate pages — we can unify everything on one comprehensive page

### MioLegale (#3)
- **Strength**: Calculator with ratings (4.8/1455), ranks well for many damage keywords
- **Weakness**: Thin content (~500 words), focused only on Tabelle Milano, NO FAQ, NO schema
- **Opportunity**: No coverage of Tabella Unica Nazionale, no Roma tables, no legal depth

### Chiarini (#6)
- **Strength**: Extremely deep legal content (~5,000+ words), excellent jurisprudence citations, well-structured sections, normativa links
- **Weakness**: NO calculator tool, NO interactive element, NO FAQ, NO data tables, NO schema markup
- **Opportunity**: This is the content depth benchmark — but without a tool it has low dwell time

### Osservatorio Amianto (#5)
- **Strength**: Comprehensive coverage of all damage types (biologico, morale, esistenziale, catastrofale, tanatologico), good legal depth (~3,500 words)
- **Weakness**: NO calculator, NO FAQ, no tables, somewhat niche (amianto focus), dated (Nov 2023)
- **Opportunity**: Good content model to emulate but needs calculator + schema + FAQ

### Risarcimenti Assicurativi (#8)
- **Strength**: Has a data table with examples (punti/età/importo), mentions both Milano and Roma tables
- **Weakness**: Thin content (~1,200 words), no calculator, generic and non-authoritative
- **Opportunity**: Low quality — easy to beat

## Calculator/Tool Recommendation

**The calculator is mandatory.** The top 3 results ALL have calculators with ratings.

### Calculator Features (must match/beat Andreani):
1. **Macropermanenti**: Punti di invalidità (10-100%) + età → danno biologico base + incremento sofferenza
2. **Micropermanenti**: Punti (1-9%) + età + giorni IT → danno biologico + temporaneo
3. **Table selection**: Tabelle Milano 2024 / Tabelle Roma / Tabella Unica Nazionale 2025
4. **Personalizzazione**: % di aumento per personalizzazione (up to 30% for Milano, 20% for micropermanenti)
5. **Invalidità temporanea**: Giorni ITT/ITP con percentuali
6. **Results**: Danno biologico permanente + danno morale/sofferenza + temporaneo = totale

### Our differentiator vs Andreani:
- **Tabella Unica Nazionale 2025** integrated (Andreani has it as separate page)
- **All three systems in one calculator** (Andreani splits micro/macro)
- **Comprehensive editorial content below** (Andreani has almost none)
- **FAQ section** (nobody has one)
- **Schema markup** for rich results
