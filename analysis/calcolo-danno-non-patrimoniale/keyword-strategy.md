## Key SEO Opportunity: Tabella Unica Nazionale 2025

**Critical finding**: D.P.R. 13 gennaio 2025, n. 12 introduced the **Tabella Unica Nazionale** for macrolesioni, effective from March 5, 2025. This is brand new legislation and many competitors haven't updated. This is our content differentiator.

The page should cover ALL three calculation systems:
1. **Micropermanenti (1-9%)**: art. 139 Codice Assicurazioni (D.Lgs. 209/2005)
2. **Macropermanenti (>9%)**: Tabelle Milano 2024 (still widely used)
3. **Tabella Unica Nazionale**: D.P.R. 12/2025 (new national standard)
