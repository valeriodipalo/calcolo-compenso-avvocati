## Strategic Assessment
- **Opportunity level**: HIGH — KD only 3, strong commercial intent, high search volume
- **Differentiation angles**:
  1. Unified calculator covering BOTH micropermanenti and macropermanenti (competitors split these)
  2. Updated TUN (Tabella Unica Nazionale 2025) integration — most competitors don't have this yet
  3. Deep editorial content with normativa links (most competitors are calculator-only)
  4. JSON-LD schema markup for rich results
  5. Both tabelle Milano AND TUN values in one page
- **Risks**: Andreani dominates with strong brand searches and ratings; need superior content depth
